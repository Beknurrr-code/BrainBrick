# SDG Alignment Statement: BrainBricks

**Addressed Goals**: 
* **SDG 4: Quality Education**
* **SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure**

**The Solution**:
BrainBricks democratizes access to advanced robotics and Artificial Intelligence. By providing an AI-native operating system that works with existing, affordable educational hardware like LEGO Spike Prime, we bridge the gap between simple play and high-level engineering. Our platform eliminates the "syntax barrier" through natural language control, allowing students to focus on logical reasoning and problem-solving.

**Impact**:
Our solution contributes to Goal 4 by providing a low-cost, scalable STEM tool that can be deployed in schools worldwide. It aligns with Goal 9 by fostering a new generation of innovators capable of building agentic systems. We aim to create a measurable impact by increasing student engagement in robotics by 40% and reducing the time required to build a functional AI-vision prototype from months to minutes.
